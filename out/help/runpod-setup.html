Step 1: Create a RunPod Account

  1. Open your web browser and go to: www.runpod.io
  2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
  3. Create an account using your email or Google/GitHub login
  4. Verify your email address (check your inbox)
  5. Log in to RunPod

Step 2: Add Credit to Your Account

  1. Once logged in, click your profile icon (top right corner)
  2. Select "Billing" from the menu
  3. Click "Add Credit" or "Add Funds"
  4. Add at least $5 (this will last many hours of voice generation)
  5. Choose your payment method and complete the payment
  6. You'll see your credit balance update
üí° Tip: $10 of credit typically lasts 15-30 hours of use depending on the GPU you choose!

Step 3: Get Your RunPod API Key

  1. Click your profile icon again (top right)
  2. Go to "Settings" or look for "API Keys" in the menu
  3. Click "Create API Key" or "+ API Key"
  4. Give it a name like: "DeepFak3r App"
  5. Click "Create" or "Generate"
  6. Your API key appears ‚Äì it looks like a long string of random letters and numbers
  7. Click "Copy" to copy it to your clipboard
  8. Save it somewhere safe! (Like a Notes app or password manager)
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Treat your API key like a password. Don't share it publicly!

Step 4: Connect RunPod in DeepFak3r Vibe Voice

  1. Open DeepFak3r Vibe Voice on your computer
  2. Look for the settings menu or gear icon (usually in the top corner)
  3. Find "Cloud Computing", "RunPod Settings", or "GPU Settings"
  4. Click the "Use Cloud Computing" or "Enable RunPod" button
  5. A text box appears asking for your API key
  6. Paste your RunPod API key (right-click ‚Üí Paste, or Ctrl+V / Cmd+V)
  7. Click "Connect", "Deploy", or "Activate"
  8. The app will set up everything automatically (this takes 2-3 minutes the first time)
  9. You'll see a status like "Connected", "Ready", or a green indicator
  10. You're all set! The app now uses RunPod's powerful computers!

How It Works

  • When you generate a voice, it's processed on RunPod's servers (not your computer)
  • Your computer just sends the text/settings and receives the finished audio
  • Everything happens automatically in the background
  • The app automatically stops the RunPod instance when you close it (so you don't waste money)

Managing Your RunPod Usage

Check your spending:

  1. Go to your RunPod dashboard
  2. Look at "Billing" or "Usage" to see how much credit you've used

Stop manually (if needed):

  • In DeepFak3r Vibe Voice, go to Cloud Settings and click "Disconnect"
  • Or close the app (it auto-disconnects)

Costs:

  • RunPod charges by the minute
  • Typical rate: $0.30-0.60 per hour depending on GPU type
  • The app shows an estimate when you connect
